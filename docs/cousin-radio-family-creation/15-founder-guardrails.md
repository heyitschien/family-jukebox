# 15 — Founder Guardrails

This document protects the heart of Cousin Radio as it grows.

## North Star

```text
Songs for the people we love.
```

If a product decision does not serve this, question it.

## What Cousin Radio Should Feel Like

- warm
- beautiful
- safe
- family-centered
- emotionally honest
- playful when appropriate
- respectful of elders and children
- simple enough for non-technical people

## What Cousin Radio Should Avoid Becoming

- a generic AI music generator
- a follower-count social network
- a content farm
- a place for public embarrassment
- a platform that exposes private family life
- a tool that encourages celebrity imitation
- a product that hides costs or privacy settings

## Founder Decision Test

Before building a major feature, ask:

1. Does this help someone love their family better?
2. Does this make creation easier without removing meaning?
3. Does this protect privacy?
4. Does this make sharing more beautiful?
5. Would a grandparent understand it?
6. Would I be proud if my family used this?

## Product Language Rules

Use warm human language.

Prefer:

```text
Create a song for someone you love
Save to family album
Share with family
Make public only if you are comfortable
```

Avoid overly technical language like:

```text
Generate asset bundle
Run model pipeline
Deploy content object
```

Technical language belongs in developer docs, not the user experience.

## Design Rules

- Large simple actions
- Clear privacy labels
- Beautiful preview before publishing
- No clutter
- Mobile-first
- Family-safe tone
- Playful but not childish
- Magical but trustworthy

## AI Rules

AI should help users express what they already feel.

AI should not pressure users to overshare, exaggerate, or create public content.

AI should preserve specificity:

- names
- memories
- places when appropriate
- inside jokes
- family details
- language and cultural flavor

But AI should also protect privacy.

## Public Sharing Rule

Private creation is the core.

Public radio is optional.

Public growth should never come before family trust.

## Monetization Rule

Charge for real value:

- better creation
- finished songs
- private albums
- long-term storage
- family sharing

Do not monetize confusion, addiction, or accidental usage.

## Final Guardrail

Cousin Radio is not about making more content.

It is about helping people say:

```text
I love you.
I remember this.
This mattered.
This is our song.
```
