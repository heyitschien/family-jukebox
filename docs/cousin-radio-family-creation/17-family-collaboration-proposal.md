# 17 — Family Collaboration Proposal

## Title

Cousin Radio: A Family-Inspired Company for Songs, Memories, and Everyday Joy

## Purpose of This Proposal

This proposal is meant to be shared with family as a clear invitation into the Cousin Radio vision.

The goal is to invite collaboration, feedback, testing, stories, and creative energy while keeping ownership, leadership, and decision-making clear.

## Opening Message

Cousin Radio started as a simple family music project, but it is becoming something bigger:

```text
Cousin Radio turns love, jokes, memories, and tiny everyday moments into songs worth sharing.
```

The original heart is still simple:

```text
Songs for the people we love.
```

It can be birthday songs, friendship songs, songs for grandparents, songs about family recipes, songs about gardens, or even silly songs about sweet potatoes.

The point is not fame. The point is connection.

## Why This Matters Now

AI has made it possible for ordinary families to create music, stories, images, and media that used to require studios, musicians, designers, engineers, and expensive production teams.

Software as a service is one layer of opportunity. But the deeper opportunity is emotional:

```text
Can families create their own private radio stations of love, memory, humor, and celebration?
```

Cousin Radio is our answer.

## What Cousin Radio Could Become

Cousin Radio could become:

- a family music studio
- a private family album host
- a place to preserve family memories
- a way to create songs for birthdays and special occasions
- a way to turn tiny everyday moments into playful songs
- a future platform other families can use
- a family-owned cultural and software project

## Recommended Company Structure

The proposed structure is:

```text
Founder-led, family-inspired, professionally operated.
```

This means:

- Chien leads product, engineering, roadmap, and business execution.
- Family participates through feedback, testing, stories, creativity, and advice.
- Roles are clear so family relationships stay protected.
- Formal ownership or compensation can be discussed later if the project becomes revenue-generating and roles become official.

## Chien's Role

Chien will serve as:

```text
Founder
CEO
Chief Product Lead
Engineering Lead
Creative Systems Lead
```

Responsibilities:

- product vision
- technical build
- app design direction
- AI pipeline strategy
- repository and deployment ownership
- provider evaluation
- legal and safety planning
- roadmap decisions
- final decision authority

## Family Collaboration Roles

Family members can contribute in ways that match their interests and strengths.

### Family Experience Advisors

Help answer:

- Does this feel warm?
- Would a parent or grandparent understand it?
- Would a family actually use this?
- Is the sharing flow comfortable?
- Does it feel safe?

### Creative Advisors

Help with:

- photography
- album covers
- visual taste
- family storytelling
- song themes
- presentation quality

### Beta Testers

Help test:

- song creation
- sharing links
- mobile experience
- family album pages
- privacy controls
- whether the songs are actually fun to send

### Story Contributors

Help provide:

- family memories
- recipes
- funny moments
- birthday stories
- garden stories
- cultural traditions
- songs for specific people

### Community Ambassadors

Help explore:

- whether other families understand the idea
- who would use it first
- what occasions matter most
- what feels confusing or magical

## Proposed Family Mastermind Format

Meeting cadence:

```text
Monthly while exploring
```

Suggested length:

```text
60 to 90 minutes
```

Meeting structure:

1. Listen to one or two new songs.
2. Review the product vision.
3. Discuss one family use case.
4. Test one feature or mockup.
5. Collect feedback.
6. Choose one next step.

## What We Are Not Deciding Yet

At the first meeting, we are not deciding:

- equity splits
- salaries
- legal entity structure
- investor plans
- full launch date
- public platform rules

Those decisions come later after validation, legal review, and clearer contribution levels.

## What We Are Deciding First

At the first meeting, we are deciding:

- whether the family loves the mission
- whether people want to help test it
- what use cases feel most alive
- who wants to participate casually
- what the first family songs or albums should be

## Clear Expectation

This is a family-inspired project, but it needs clear leadership.

The current proposal:

```text
Chien leads and owns the execution.
Family advises, tests, contributes stories, and helps shape the emotional direction.
```

If the company grows into revenue, formal roles, investment, or employment, we will create written agreements before changing expectations.

## Invitation

I would love to invite the family into this as a creative council and early testing group.

The first goal is not to make everything official immediately.

The first goal is to ask:

```text
Can this help families express love, memory, humor, and celebration in a new way?
```

If the answer is yes, then we build patiently, clearly, and beautifully.

## Closing Thought

Cousin Radio may become software.

It may become a company.

But at its deepest level, it can become a family heirloom:

```text
A living archive of songs for the people we love.
```
