# 01 — Product Vision

## One-Line Vision

Cousin Radio helps families turn memories, love, and spoken stories into beautiful songs they can save, share, and return to for years.

## Why This Matters

Most music platforms are built for artists and listeners.

Cousin Radio is built for families and relationships.

The emotional job is not “stream music.” The emotional job is:

> “I want to give someone I love a song that says what I feel.”

That is the unique wedge.

## Product Promise

Cousin Radio should let a person do this without needing to understand songwriting, prompting, audio production, hosting, file formats, GitHub, Vercel, or AI pipelines.

The user should only need to:

1. choose the occasion,
2. speak the story,
3. preview the song,
4. save it,
5. share it.

## Primary Use Cases

### Birthday Song

A son creates a song for his mom.

### Anniversary Song

A spouse creates a private love song.

### Grandma / Grandpa Song

Children capture memories with elders while they are still here.

### Friendship Song

A friend celebrates another friend.

### Family Recipe Song

A family turns a dish, garden, or kitchen memory into a playful track.

### Baby / Graduation Song

A family marks a life milestone.

## Product Values

- Uplifting by default
- Family-safe by design
- Simple enough for grandparents
- Magical enough for children
- Private unless intentionally shared
- Human story first, AI second
- No pressure to become an influencer
- No dark engagement loops
- Built for love, not addiction

## The Main Button

The entire future product can begin with one primary button:

```text
✨ Create Family Song
```

Secondary options:

```text
Birthday
Thank You
Anniversary
Friendship
For Grandma / Grandpa
For Kids
Garden / Food / Home
Custom
```

## Privacy Modes

Every song should have a clear privacy state:

```text
Private Draft
Family Only
Invite Link
Public on Cousin Radio
```

Default should be **Private Draft** or **Family Only**.

## Public Cousin Radio

Public discovery should feel curated and safe:

- Birthday Radio
- Grandma Radio
- Garden Radio
- Friendship Radio
- New Baby Radio
- Immigration Stories Radio
- Songs for the People We Love

Public publishing should require clear consent from the creator and a moderation pass.

## What Cousin Radio Is Not

Cousin Radio is not trying to replace Spotify, YouTube, TikTok, or professional artists.

It is not a fame machine.

It is a memory-and-love machine.

## Success Definition

The product is working when someone hears a song made for them and says:

> “I can’t believe you made this for me.”

That moment is the core product metric.
