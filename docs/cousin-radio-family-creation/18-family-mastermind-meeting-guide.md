# 18 — Family Mastermind Meeting Guide

This document is a practical guide for the first Cousin Radio family meeting.

## Meeting Goal

Introduce Cousin Radio as a founder-led, family-inspired company and invite family collaboration without creating confusion around ownership, authority, or expectations.

## Suggested Meeting Length

```text
75 minutes
```

## Suggested Attendees

- Chien
- Eve
- mother-in-law
- father-in-law
- sisters-in-law
- trusted family members or friends who understand the spirit of the project

## Meeting Tone

Warm, clear, playful, and grounded.

This is not a pitch to investors.

This is a family conversation about a possible family-inspired company and creative project.

## Pre-Meeting Prep

Prepare:

- Cousin Radio open on phone or laptop
- one serious song example
- one funny song example, such as Sweet Potato Soul
- product direction sentence
- simple explanation of the current manual pipeline
- this meeting guide
- family collaboration proposal

## Agenda

### 1. Opening — 5 minutes

Say:

```text
I want to share Cousin Radio clearly today. I see this as a founder-led, family-inspired project. I will lead product and engineering, but I want the family involved as creative advisors, testers, storytellers, and early supporters.
```

### 2. Product Direction — 5 minutes

Share:

```text
Cousin Radio turns love, jokes, memories, and tiny everyday moments into songs worth sharing.
```

Then share the north star:

```text
Songs for the people we love.
```

### 3. Demo — 10 minutes

Show:

- the Cousin Radio app
- the current homepage
- one family-style song
- one playful everyday song
- how a link appears when shared by text

Ask:

```text
Does this feel like something a family would actually send to each other?
```

### 4. Explain the Opportunity — 10 minutes

Explain simply:

```text
AI is making it possible for normal families to create songs, images, and memories without needing a studio. The opportunity is not just AI music. The opportunity is private family media creation.
```

### 5. Explain the Structure — 10 minutes

Say:

```text
I want to keep the company founder-led so decisions stay clear. I will lead product, engineering, roadmap, and execution. I want the family involved through feedback, stories, testing, creative direction, and emotional wisdom.
```

Clarify:

```text
We are not deciding equity or compensation today. If this becomes revenue-generating or someone takes on a formal role, we will create written agreements before changing expectations.
```

### 6. Family Feedback — 20 minutes

Ask:

- What kinds of songs would your family actually create?
- Who would you send a song to first?
- Would you use this for birthdays?
- Would you use this for funny inside jokes?
- What would make you nervous about privacy?
- What would make this feel more beautiful?
- Would grandparents understand it?
- What should Cousin Radio never become?

### 7. Role Interest — 10 minutes

Ask:

```text
Who wants to help casually as a tester, story contributor, creative advisor, or family experience advisor?
```

Make it low pressure.

### 8. Next Steps — 5 minutes

Choose one or two next steps:

- create a family birthday song
- create a funny everyday anthem
- test the share flow
- collect song ideas
- meet again next month

## Important Boundaries

Do not let the first meeting become:

- an equity negotiation
- a debate about every feature
- a legal formation meeting
- a pressure session for unpaid work
- a public launch planning meeting

Keep it focused on vision, feedback, and family alignment.

The best first meeting outcome is not a final business structure.

