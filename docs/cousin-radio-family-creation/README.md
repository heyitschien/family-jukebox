# Cousin Radio Family Creation Platform

**North Star:** Songs for the people we love.

Cousin Radio can grow from a beautiful family jukebox into a family music creation and hosting platform: a place where any family can speak a memory, generate a song, save it into a family album, and share it privately or publicly.

The product should stay emotionally focused: love, memory, gratitude, celebration, family, friendship, children, elders, gardens, birthdays, anniversaries, migrations, meals, faith, and everyday joy.

## The Core Experience

1. Tap **Create Family Song**.
2. Speak naturally: “I want a birthday song for my mom. Her name is Linda. She loves gardening, roses, and Vietnamese food. She always took care of us growing up. Make it joyful and bilingual.”
3. Cousin Radio transcribes the voice note.
4. AI extracts the story, people, occasion, tone, privacy setting, and safety notes.
5. AI creates lyrics, music, cover art, metadata, and an album entry.
6. The family reviews and saves.
7. The song appears in the family album and can be shared.

## Product Shift

Current model:

```text
One family manually creates songs and publishes them through the current pipeline.
```

Future model:

```text
Every family can create, save, and share songs through Cousin Radio with minimal technical friction.
```

This turns Cousin Radio into:

- a private family radio station,
- a memory archive,
- an AI-assisted songwriting studio,
- a family album host,
- and eventually a public uplifting radio network.

## Direction Decision

```text
Private creation is the foundation.
Public celebration is optional.
```

Cousin Radio should become private-first as soon as practical: public landing page, protected family content, and optional public radio for explicitly approved songs.

## First Build Principle

Do not begin with a giant platform.

Begin with one magical button:

> **I want to make a song for someone I love.**

Everything else grows from that.
